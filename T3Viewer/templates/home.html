All entries must be n tag:value format - one per line.
The allowed tags are:
  • email: e.g test2@d2.officeburrito.com
  • timestamp or Date/Time or Launch Time: e.g 2015-06-04T19:12:20.404Z
  • userid: eg. us-east-1:7df6e942-10c5-431f-9258-772e0b937ccd
  • deviceid or Device Id: Ncho39D6C69AXBC8EX4C81XBEB0X
  • span: e.g 2

Note: For faster searches, provide the deviceid along with the userid.

Email search examples: 
email:user@example.com
timestamp: 2015-06-03T15:14:30.425Z

email:@example.com
timestamp:now
span:32

email:6a5fff1d2d576dfa3886537f1450d8659359d0c56cb04b35cc52197b61d58222@nac02.com
If timestamp is omitted, latest entry is used.
